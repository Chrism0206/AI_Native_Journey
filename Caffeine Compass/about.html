About Caffeine Compass

Discovering your perfect coffee, one bean at a time

Our Mission

Caffeine Compass is an AI-powered coffee bean recommender that helps you discover your perfect coffee based on flavor preferences and roast level. We believe everyone deserves to find their ideal cup of coffee, and we're here to guide you on that journey.

How It Works

Our sophisticated algorithm analyzes multiple dimensions of coffee preferences including roast level compatibility, flavor profile matching, acidity level alignment, and body preference matching to provide you with personalized recommendations.
